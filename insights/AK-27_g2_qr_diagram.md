# AK-27 · G₂ and the QR diagram

## Approach tried

QR diagram of velocity-gradient invariants ($Q = (|S|^2 -
|\omega|^2)/2$, $R = \det(S) - \text{coupling}$) interpreted as
$G_2$-symmetric.

## Obstruction

QR diagram is the **2D discriminant locus of $A_2 =
\mathfrak{sl}(3, \mathbb{R})$**, not a $G_2$-symmetric object.
$G_2$ requires 7-dim manifold; QR is 2D.  Fundamental reason:
NS algebraic home is $A_2$.  Cartan $\mathfrak{sl}(3, \mathbb{R})
= \mathfrak{so}(3) \oplus \mathrm{sym}_0(3)$ IS the strain-
vorticity split.  Proof succeeds via dimensional reduction to
1D scale variable $\rho$ (rank-1 $A_1$).  Exceptional algebras
(rank $\geq 2$) **resist dimensional reduction** — opposite of
what the proof exploits.
