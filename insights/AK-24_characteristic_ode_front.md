# AK-24 · Characteristic ODE along parabolic front

## Approach tried

Track hat-function energy along $\gamma = \{(\rho, s^*(\rho))\}$
in $(\rho, s)$ plane.  Total derivative
$(\partial_\rho + (ds^*/d\rho)\partial_s) E_s(\rho)$ along $\gamma$
supposedly satisfies ODE with $\beta > 1$, $\alpha < 1$.

## Obstruction

Temporal contribution $\partial_s E_s(\rho)$ involves third
derivatives of $v$ + pressure; no favorable sign.  No
characteristic curve $s^*(\rho)$ found that produces the
required exponents.  Remains speculative / open; no explicit
closed-form yet demonstrated.
