# AK-16 · Helical-symmetry Liouville in L^{3,∞}

## Approach tried

Use helical symmetry $SO(2)$ action to extract a stronger
Liouville theorem in $L^{3,\infty}$.

## Obstruction

No helical $L^{3,\infty}$ Liouville exists.  The KNSS ancient
solution carries no helical symmetry.  KNSS proved $v = 0$ for
axi-symmetric no-swirl (scalar maximum principles on
$\omega_\theta/r$ and $r \cdot u_\theta$).  Helical direction
is intermediate — no existing regularity result covers it.
Symmetry reduction is unavailable; the proof must treat general
3D via INS-02 + ESS03.
